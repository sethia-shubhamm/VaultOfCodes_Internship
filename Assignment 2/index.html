Dessert

Chocolate
Lava Cake

⏱ 25 min Total Time
🔥 10 min Prep Time
🍽 4 Servings
📊 Easy Difficulty
Step 1 of 6 0%
⏳ Cooking timer: 15:00

Ingredients

  • 113g dark chocolate (70% cocoa)
  • 113g unsalted butter
  • 2 whole eggs
  • 2 egg yolks
  • 100g powdered sugar
  • 30g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • pinch sea salt
  • for dusting cocoa powder (ramekins)

Instructions

  1. 01

    Prep ramekins

    Butter four 6-oz ramekins generously and dust with cocoa powder, tapping out any excess. Place them on a baking tray and set aside.

  2. 02

    Melt chocolate & butter

    Combine the dark chocolate and butter in a heatproof bowl. Melt together over a double boiler or in 30-second microwave bursts, stirring until fully smooth. Let cool slightly.

  3. 03

    Whisk eggs & sugar

    In a separate bowl, whisk together the eggs, egg yolks, and powdered sugar until the mixture is pale and slightly thickened — about 2 minutes by hand.

  4. 04

    Combine & fold flour

    Pour the melted chocolate mixture into the egg mixture and whisk to combine. Add vanilla extract, then sift in the flour and salt. Fold gently until just incorporated — do not overmix.

  5. 05

    Fill & chill

    Divide the batter evenly among the prepared ramekins. At this point you can refrigerate them for up to 24 hours. Bring back to room temperature 20 minutes before baking.

  6. 06

    Bake & serve

    Preheat oven to 220°C (425°F). Bake for 12–14 minutes until the edges are set but the centre still jiggles. Run a knife around the edge, invert onto a plate, and serve immediately with vanilla ice cream.
